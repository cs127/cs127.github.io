info / about me

hi! I'm cs, a music producer, programmer, and minecraft player.
I'm 19 years old, and I go by any pronouns, but I prefer they/them.

I can speak English, Persian, and toki pona[→].
and I am currently learning Japanese.

I was born on 2005-04-04 (4th of April 2005), in Tehran, Iran.
I currently still live there, but I will most likely leave as soon as possible.

I started experimenting with music when I was 5 years old.
most songs that I made until 2020 were MIDI files.
my first proper song was an XG MIDI version of "16-bit"[→], which I made in 2019.
in late 2020, I started using OpenMPT[→] to make my songs, which is now my main choice for music production.
in fact, I became so obsessed with OpenMPT that I wrote a part of its file format documentation[→]. :3

most of my music is inspired by synthwave and 1980s synthpop,
but I also sometimes make chiptune[→] and demoscene[→]-style music.

as for programming, I started learning the very basics in 2013.
my main language was BASIC, until I learnt a bit of Java in 2020, and later, C, in 2022.
I also know a little bit of Python, but not as much as C and Java.

that's about it, if you have any questions, feel free to contact me!
